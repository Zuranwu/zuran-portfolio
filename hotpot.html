Introduction to Sichuan Hot Pot

The Charm of Sichuan Hot Pot

Sichuan Hot Pot, as one of the four major hot pots in China, is famous for its spicy and fragrant taste. It is not only a delicacy but also a culture and a way of life.

The base ingredient of Sichuan Hot Pot is usually boiled with chili peppers, Sichuan peppers, beef fat, and various spices, resulting in a rich and varied flavor. During the cooking process, various ingredients such as meat, seafood, and vegetables are boiled in the rolling hot pot, fully absorbing the aroma of the base ingredient, leaving a lingering taste.

Whether in cold winter or hot summer, Sichuan Hot Pot can bring people endless gustatory enjoyment. It not only satisfies people's pursuit of delicacies but also lets them feel the enthusiasm and hospitality of the Sichuan people.
